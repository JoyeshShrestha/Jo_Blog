21st September, 2022
To understand why butterflies are often associated with death, you need to know the basics of their developmental process. A butterfly hatches from an egg and grows into a caterpillar. As a caterpillar, it goes into a chrysalis and undergoes a transformative process. It emerges from its chrysalis as a beautiful winged butterfly. Many cultures look at this process with deeper meaning and compare a human’s spiritual life with this transformation. To many, the butterfly symbolizes renewal, hope, endurance, resurrection, and the transformation into a better being.
